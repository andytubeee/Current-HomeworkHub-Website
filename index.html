About Us:

Hi, we're the HomeworkHub, a youth orchestrated free after school tutoring club! HomeworkHub provides Elementary school students with FREE tutoring sessions and volunteer tutors with community service hours, all from the comfort and safety of your own homes!
We understand how hard it is for younger students to adapt and keep up with the virtual and distance learning methods. We also know how limited and difficult volunteer opportunities are to find for those who want to help the community. We are dedicated to helping people who are currently facing those problems through our services! Together, we can work towards improving the community and getting through these tough times!

Why You Should Join

Are you looking to work with younger children and help our community while gaining volunteer hours? Under current circumstances, it's difficult to find ways to get Volunteer Hours as all the usual institutions are either closed or fully booked. By joining HomeworkHub, not only are you assisting someone that might be struggling with school, earning volunteer hours, AND building up your university resume, but you are also given the opportunity to develop patience, leadership and communication skills all while staying safe!

Task Description

After the application process, you will be paired with an elementary student and have 45 minute online tutoring sessions with them over a span of 8 weeks.

During These Session You Will Be:

  • Tutoring your students on your assigned subjects
  • Assisting them with any questions or assignments they may have
  • Logging your time!

Requirements

  • At least 14 years old or be in high school
  • Commitmentment and dedication to the task
  • Must be comfortable working with kids and pre-teens
  • Good grades and communication skills are necessary!

Our Mission:

With the world currently in a pandemic and thousands of schools closed or modified, many students are forced to learn online or through a hybrid curriculum. Many children have had difficulties adjusting or keeping up with this new method of learning. There just isn't enough assistance to go around to help all students thrive in this virtual environment. We at HomeworkHub recognize the demand for extra assistance are committed to delivering free personalized online tutoring sessions to students in need of assistance.

With the Pandemic also comes the closing of many volunteer and community organizations as well. As a group of high school students, we also found it very difficult to help our community during these difficult times and earn volunteer hours as well. That's why we began HomeworkHub. Apart from providing free and accessible help for young students during these unprecedented times, HomeworkHub is dedicated to providing an outlet for students passionate about our community to help others, earn valuable experiences, and fill their volunteer hours.

Frequently Asked Questions:

Can I sign up for more than one subject?

Absolutely! You can sign up for a total of 3 subjects at a time, although tutor availability may vary from subject to subject.

My 8 weeks are up. Can I sign up again?

Yes! You can sign up as many times as you want! Again, availability may vary.

My Tutor/Student is unresponsive, what do I do?

Please email us! We'll get in touch with the person and sort it out. If they are still unresponsive, we'll assign you to someone new!

I finished my sessions! What do I do now?

Congratulations! Log in your hours and we'll contact you when we have someone new for you!

I signed up for HomeworkHub but no one has been in touch?

Please check your spam inbox! Our emails sometimes wind up in there. If you're still having problems, feel free to email us and we'll sort it out!
